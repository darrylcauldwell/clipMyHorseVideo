ClearRound makes it easy to combine your showjumping clips into one polished video.

Pick your clips from your photo library, arrange them in order, trim the start and end of each one, then export a single merged video ready to share.

Features:
- Import multiple video clips from your photo library
- Drag to reorder clips on the timeline
- Trim each clip with a visual filmstrip editor
- Add crossfade transitions between clips
- Choose export quality to balance file size and detail
- Share directly to social media, messaging apps, or save to your library

Built for riders, grooms, and equestrian fans who want to share their rounds without fiddly editing software.
